Welcome to the MonoMuse Museum

MonoMuse is a contemporary museum dedicated to exploring the intersection of art, technology, and human creativity.

Plan your visit to experience cutting-edge exhibitions, hands-on experiences, and events designed to inspire curiosity and creativity.

Discover how MonoMuse blends immersive exhibitions with hands-on technology and digital storytelling.

Through rotating collections, interactive installations, and community events, we invite you to see how innovation shapes culture. Members and first-time visitors alike find new angles on creativity—from gallery talks to maker labs and evening programs.
